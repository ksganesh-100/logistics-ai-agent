Call-Off Process Flow

1. Request Call Off

Customer initiates the call-off request via email or portal input.

2. AI Agent Parses Email

Email Intake Agent extracts data, creates call-off draft in Logistics App.

3. Contract Validation

Validator Agent checks contract validity and quota availability.

4. Inventory Assignment

Assignment Agent identifies suitable batch to fulfill call off.

5. Ops Approval

Ops Routing Agent sends fulfillment plan to operations team for confirmation.

6. Warehouse & Carrier Prep

Warehouse prepares goods, carrier confirms pickup and loads cargo.

7. Document Upload

Packing List, CMR, and POD are uploaded and validated in the system.

8. AI Tracker & Exception Handler

Agents monitor call-off status, flag delays, and raise alerts if necessary.

9. Movement Completion

Delivery completed. Call-off marked as closed, documents archived.
